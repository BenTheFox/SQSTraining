\FeatureLoader

The FeatureLoader is responsible for allowing the serving of "bugged" or "errored" pages to a user. When one of these pages, or "features" is requested, the database is queried to determine which version of a feature to serve.

The version of a feature to serve is dependent on the user's unique ID.
